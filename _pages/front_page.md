# Undergraduate at the University of Michigan:
I am in my 4th year of undergrad studying Robotics Engineering. I will be graduating in May and plan to pursue full time employment in the industry before coming back for graduate education in a few years.

# Research
Over the past year I had the pleasure of working in the [Barton Research Group](http://brg.engin.umich.edu/) where I worked in the groups smart manufacturing test bed to integrate a [Spot](http://bostondynamics.com/products/spot/) robot into the test bed.

# RUSC
As a founding member and current President of RUSC (The Robotics Undergraduate Student Council), I work with the robotics department to promote professional development opportunities for robotics students as well as promote RUSC's greater goals of representing the undergraduate population in the department and encouraging outreach in the wider robotics community

For more info on RUSC see: [The RUSC Webpage](http://robotics.umich.edu/academics/current-students/robotics-undergraduate-student-council/)
